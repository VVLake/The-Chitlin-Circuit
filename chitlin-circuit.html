Welcome to The Chitlin Circuit

Where Legacy Meets the Spotlight

Honoring History, Celebrating Talent

The Chitlin Circuit is a state-of-the-art performing arts venue inspired by the legendary network of Black-owned stages that gave rise to icons during the Jim Crow era. Our venue proudly continues this tradition of excellence, diversity, and empowerment.

  • Luxury seating with clear views from every seat
  • Fine dining experience with curated Southern cuisine
  • Accessible for both rising and established artists
